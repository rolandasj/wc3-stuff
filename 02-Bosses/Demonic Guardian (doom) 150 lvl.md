You need complete quest chain to unlock him. It starts in wastelands town in a cave.
He will tell you to active generators.
- 1st is located right of dremora gardens
- 2nd is in plague lands
- 3rd is in wild lands close to bandit cemetery
- 4th is temple of annouk
- 5th is in ice fortress
- 6th is in demon atoll
Now return back to quest npc. You will get Plasma Energy. You need to open the entrance to the boss.
The entrance is in demon atoll.
