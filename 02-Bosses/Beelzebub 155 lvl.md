Start quest in secret lab, take item and bring it to capital. Go to the 1st purple portal in the capital and talk to Rufus. Go to the 2nd purple portal in the capital and Find frozen npc.
Go to ice fort and enter the secret area, you can walk up on top of terrain. Kill Arthas. Destroy the box. Take the item and go back to 2nd purple portal to unfreeze the npc. Talk to him and he should teleport out.
Go back to 1st purple capital portal and talk to Rufus. It should unlock Beelzebub boss
