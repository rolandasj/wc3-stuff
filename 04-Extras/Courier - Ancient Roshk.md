Courier drops from Kransparus boss, to reach him you need do quest line in Ice fort starting by killing 45 trolls.
Boss should respawn if you complete the quest after first kill

- increases attack power 100%  - Fighting Spirit Aura
- increases ranged damage100% - Accuracy Aura 
- Reduces the combat speed by 5% of nearby enemies - Ice Tornado debuff
- Grants 10% dodge in a 500 radius - Ice Cover aura
