In order to get the courier you have to kill Blood Queen boss.
After you kill her a message will appear to find the carpet.
Carpet is in wastelands.
With the lamp you can choose to get ginny courier.
To get buffs you need to get a 2nd lamp. Equip on hero and write 'rub' a few times until buff menu shows up.
Current courier page should not have any items otherwise you will loose them.
you cannot have multiple same buffs, dont stack.
Possible buffs:
- Mag. boost +10% of maximum value
- Physical gain +10% of the maximum value
- 1000 HP regeneration units
- 90 units of protection
- 15% dodge
- Life restoration for 30k. Cooldown 20s, mana costs 3000
- Mana restoration by 15k. Cooldown 60s, costs 3000 health
- Increase gold gain from kills by 2 times
- Poisoning, allows you to deal an additional 20% of the damage dealt
- Saves from death with a 20% chance

Once wishes chosen, you cannoy undo or change them, you have to redo the whole thing again.
