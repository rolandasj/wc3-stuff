## Enchanted Guardian of the Grove - Third Guardian
Kill 3rd Guardian (Located in level 1 Troll Forest on big Green Circle surrounded by 3 Green Spheres).
3rd key and charge it in Machinarium (Located in Machinarium to the left behind textures).
## Spiritual Elder - Second Guardian 
Get quest from Orc Warlock in Fort of Jacob. Talk to Kobolt in Kobolt Mine.
Deliver Meliolit and Virt Leg to Kobolt to receive Scroll.
Deliver Scroll to Orc Warlock.
Talk to him more and he will need open demon portal, but you need escort him. You need write '-ready' for him to teleport and start escort.
Escort him to middle of undead area (right of Draenai Gardens). Once at the portal talk to him, he will open it.
Kill the Guardian inside portal to get second key.
Second key can be charged in top right of level 35 Orc area (in secret path through water).
## Ancient Guardian of the Wasteland - First Guardian
Talk to the kid (located above King in Capital). After talking to kid, go left in the forest until you reach river (kid's ghost will be found there).
Talk to kid ghost and he will give you scroll to deliver to crying girl located top right in Fort of Jacob. 
Return to kid ghost and talk to him. After talking to him toy will spawn a bit to the top behind the tree, from your current position (where you stand to talk to kid ghost). 
Deliver the toy to kid in Capital. Kid will walk away to Orc tower located to the right edge in Capital.
Follow him there, wait 1 minute and talk to him again.
He will then walk to Villager located left of fountain in Capital. 
Give Villager barrel of ale (drops from Bandit Lord located left of Cenarius Statue in Troll Forest level 1). 
write -+ (not sure if does something maybe skip dialogue)
After go to Archeology area and talk to the tree (located above the portal when you are in Archeology area).
After that go to Plaguelands. Before reaching shipyard there is Naga Siren npc left of it in water.
Talk to her and she will give you a Seal that you need to charge by killing 100 golems in Temple of Annouk. 
After charging it, return to Naga Siren to deliver the quest. 
Go to north of wastelands (Centaur Outpost), all the way to the top of area you will find 1st Guardian.
First you need kill 2 other mini boses starting on the left then right then he spawns top.
Kill him and get the key. First key can be charged in Archeology area (bottom left in location).
## Ancient Captive - Fourth Guardian
Bring all 3 charged keys to the gate in West Lake (above water elemental boss, top right in that area). Open the gate and kill First boss. After killing him the 4th Guardian will spawn. Kill him and return to Tree in Archeology area.
You need kill mini boss to spawn him. Mini boss gos inv very often so you need good damage and good survivability.
Guardian itself has 3 phases where he goes inv and summons a mob that is inv. If you dont kill the mob in 15 seconds or so the boss fully heals.
To make mob vuln, you need to kill supreme necros 35 times.
After that holy spirit npc will talk with you in capital in corner.
He will give you key to Malladar. His entrance is in west lake, use the key to open the doors.
To enter you need write 'entrance'.
Malladar respawns, so kill him until you have 3 items. Each use will destroy the item, This item will make the mob vuln.
Mob spawns always in the same place between 2 black lanterns. There are 3 phases so 3 places. 
You basically need minimum 2 people in order to kill him, because the last spawns far away and if boss chases you he will move too far and rest.
The items to make mob vuln save.
