Bring 3 keys here and you will get this recipe - Book of Drek-Thar 7
You have to return all quests before you try to use the keys.
## Cold Key
In the Ice Fortress, stand in the very bottom-left corner and look along the bottom wall to the right with your mouse. Find the barrel containing this key.
## Materia Key (The key of unification) 
Dropped by the Soulless Beast (appears after opening the moon gate to the secret lab). (The Moon Key drops from the Demon Worshipper.)
## Shaman's Key (The key to the swamp tomb)
Dropped by the Shaman during the quest "Shaman" (The chain to it starts from the Orc Towers), after you return orc chieftain quest, go to wasteland, go left to the next camp, npc will give quest to kill shaman. Shaman is in 33 orc camp (where chieftain was), but on the left on the hill theres portal, go in it. Boss is in that area.
