Start in fairytale forest. Talk to npc, bring back item to capital king.
Go to fairytale forest futher in a camp. Talk to npc close to doors. Kill 20 inhabitants and return. Doors should open.
Talk with some npc kellari or something. Next go back to capital and talk to king.
Now it should tell you to kill 25 crypt fiends and bloodsucker, they are located in 40-50 dead forest. Blood sucker appears after you have killed 25 mobs. Return to king
Now it should tell you go to autumn village and talk to fire. Talk to fire, it should tell you kill 15 zombies in plague land. Return.
Now it should tell you go talk to provision npc in capital. Go back to fire and after go back and talk to king.
It should tell to talk to spiritual guide or something. You need do the kill nerub quest before you can talk to it.
Go talk to far seer and it should ping to collect 6 runes. Collect and return, it tells you go back to King. Wait for scroll, bring scroll to king.
Talk to king, it tells you go talk to kellari (in a camp where doors opened). After you talk she dissapears.
She appears in plague lands, talk to it. Tells you to active the obelisks. There are 4 obelisks to activate. Must be done in order.
- 1st in 135 (3rd purple capital portal)
- 2nd in capital centre
- 3rd 15-20 bandit cemetery, secret entrance. Theres lika a riddle/maze. I went always left and i ended up reaching the obelisk
- 4th at lich
Go back to kellari, he say go to king, talk to king.
He tell you to kill sargeras generals
Satan is in 115-120 area, you need boat to sail there. Do the racoon quest to get to him.
Return to king, now he wants us to attack undead legions. Go talk to Kellari in plague lands.
Go to machinarum and kill Technoid. After go to King.
He says go fight demons, go to 112-115 area, use activator key and go fight guldan.
After start you die and go back to king. Now you actually fight guldan. Theres barrier but you should be able to go in.
After you kill, you go further in portal and you fight Kil-jaeden.
- He turn black, dont attack him run around. Not exactly sure how it works.
- Kill question marks that spawn, it heals boss.

Main story ends here on normal difficulty

On hell sargeras opens up, also santa and whatnot.
After killing Kil-jaeden, sargeras portal is open, go and kill it.
After go back capital and talk to paladin (im not sure about this part)
Killing sargeras you get achiv, which opens up underworld, the last end game area and to continue leveling to 170.
Portal to underworld can be found in 125 orc area (ashenvale forest into orc camp, there portal to the higher orc area)

For sven and whanot...
After go to the tree of wisdom above archeology town, he will talk about 3 keys of Sven generals.
He will give you 1st key. And will tell you find demon in fairy forest.
You need bring him beer or mug (forgot actual name) from Royal Crypt Guardian.
Next you need find 2nd key, it is located in Queen of the Crimson Spider lair, south wall.
3rd key is located in western lace north, close to iceberg that is visable, not far off from 4th guardian gates.
Go back to tree of wisdom. And now tell you to use the keys to each generals.
one in wastelands and 2 before bandit boss.
