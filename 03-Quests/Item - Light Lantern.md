In Dremora Garden, do the provision quest. After taking the quest return to capital, it will tell you to say command to start, the wagon should arrive to you soon enough, it will follow you. You need walk back to Dremora Garden with the wagon to finish the quest.

Upon finishing, you will get Light Lantern item.
