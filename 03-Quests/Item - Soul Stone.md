You need be level one to start the quests.
Talk to ghost. He want The Soul of the Unburied.
Die until you drop the Soul.
Go back to ghost and talk, this time normal hero can continue the questline by taking the soul.
You need talk to the oracle. Oracle is located in secret pathaway, its open new, from kobold mines at bigger kobold boss.
Talk to Oracle, should give you Soul Stone (needed for Midas Glove)
