You start the quest in 112-115 demon atoll. The hunter will ask you to bring him some meat.
To get meat you need to get butchers axe from Butcher Joseph.
Go to capital, equip the axe and kill the cows. It should drop some meat. You may need to take the quest for a special cow to spawn the one that drops it.
It has few parts with meat, but in order to continue down the quest chain, you need to deliver fried meat.
Now we need salt and spices, we can get it from barrel in 4th portal in temple of annoyk. The destructible spawns when you go close to it and has 25% to drop it. so need remakes...
Go to fire and cook it. Give the meat to the hunter.
Wait a bit and talk again, he will tell you go to Darius tower and find a story or whatever. Walk all over all corners until some text triggers, theres 3 possible places.
After text was trigered go to library and talk to keeper of knowledge.
He will tell you to get fragment from bloodsucker on diff 9. He is unlocked very quickly on the Main story quest line.
Go back and talk to keeper, now tells you talk to blacksmith. He is located in fort jacob.
He tell you to kill black drakes on diff 8. They are located in secret lab 140-148 area.
Return to him. Now he wants you to kill doom on diff 8 and find black mass.
black mass is in plague lands where nerub spiders are
blackness of doom is from doom boss. Must be diff 8+
After you got both return to blacksmith. He will tell you go to secret lab and find Holy Spirit Patron.
He will tell you go back to library and talk to keeper.
Tells you to get stone from Azrael on diff 9. This may be not soloable or barely.
He is the mini boss before 4th Guardian. You need kill all 3 guardians get keys, charge them and unlock the doors.
Kill, get stone and return to the library keeper. He will tell you go to blacksmith in fort jacob

Spices and Salt does not save.
Elwing's Tear does not save
