You need to do several quests prior to the correct one. Locked behind them.
First you need to do quest from druid. Collect 4 things in gold mine. Return the quest, it will unlock few more quests in ashenvale.
In ashenvale do those quests. The tree eventually will give you quest to bring termine 1 and axe from owl boss. Upon finish it will give you the grandma's pantaloon recipe 4
